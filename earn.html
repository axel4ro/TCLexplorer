Path 1 - Creator income

Creator Program: earn 40% revenue share in euro

If you already make content on TikTok, YouTube, Twitch, Kick, Trovo, Discord, Instagram or Facebook, this is the easiest earning lane to understand. You get a Creator Code, your audience uses it when they create an account, and you receive 40% of the card-payment revenue generated by those players.

  • Your audience gets a Starter Pack with +9 items when they use your code.
  • You get a dedicated dashboard with live tracking for signups, purchases and total revenue.
  • Payout requests start at 50 EUR and can be withdrawn weekly.
  • Payment methods include Wise, PayPal and bank transfer.
  • PopuGames handles self-billing and operates the program under EU legal coverage.
Path 2 - TCL crypto

Play, stake, sell items and earn TCL on-chain

If you are a player, collector, guild leader or trader, you can earn TCL directly through game activity and Web3 tools. Some methods are active and reward daily play. Others are passive and reward long-term conviction.

  • Use NFTs to earn daily rewards with active play or passive lending.
  • Open a Private Web3 Shop and sell classic tradable items for TCL.
  • Invite players with your referral code and earn from their purchases.
  • Use xPortal as your wallet for TCL purchases and rewards.
  • Keep TCL, sell it later, use it in Infinity Staking, or spend it on game utility such as Karma and other items.

Private Web3 Shops

How selling items for TCL works

This is the core crypto earning loop for item sellers. You use a Private Shop Web3 item, list classic tradable items for TCL, and buyers pay from xPortal or another supported wallet.

1

Open your shop

Buy the Private Shop Web3 item with Karma, place it on the map and list the classic tradable items you want to sell for TCL.

2

Buyer pays with xPortal

The buyer connects a wallet such as xPortal, confirms the TCL payment on-chain and locks the item during checkout.

3

Item goes to mailbox

After a successful purchase, the buyer receives the item through mailbox delivery while the shop handles the blockchain settlement.

4

Seller receives TCL

The seller receives TCL directly in the connected wallet and can keep it, sell it later, stake it, or use it on game utility such as Karma and other items.

Official Web3 Shop distribution: 90% goes to the seller, 4% is burned, 2% goes back to the buyer as cashback, 2% goes to the buyer referral if one was used, and 2% goes to the server wallet for blockchain network fees. If a transaction does not complete, the item becomes available again after 3 minutes and token recovery is available after 5 minutes.
